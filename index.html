WHO IS BOBBI JAY?

BOBBI JAY is a versatile artist known for their eclectic mix of musical genres, with a primary focus on tech house. Their dynamic performances have made them a prominent figure in local music scenes, where they've earned recognition for their captivating sets. With a knack for blending diverse sounds seamlessly, they've also had the honor of opening for several moderately big acts, showcasing their talent and contributing to the vibrant music community.
